Investment Proposal

Zenova Educations is a hybrid education infrastructure company focused on transforming access to quality competitive education for students across Tier-2 and Tier-3 Karnataka.

The organization combines centralized online teaching systems, offline mentorship, digital classrooms, and scalable institutional collaborations to deliver affordable and high-quality academic ecosystems for PU, KCET, NEET, and other competitive examinations.

Market Opportunity

Educational Gap

Scalable Demand

Demand for integrated PU + KCET/NEET systems continues to increase rapidly across regional Karnataka markets.

Hybrid Future

The hybrid education model enables centralized quality teaching while maintaining lower infrastructure and expansion costs.
